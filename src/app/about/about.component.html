About PartyLine

Now more than ever it's important to stay connected with loved ones and friends. A study shows that adults with a healthy social life were healthier in old age and had a lower risk of mortality, and it's being discovered that in America, loneliness can cost 15 years of life expectancy!

We care about those close to us, but it can be challenging to find time to keep up with what is going on in their lives. PartyLine helps you stay connected by automatically prompting everyone in your Party for updates on a set timeframe, and sending a newsletter out to each member!

You define who is in your party, and can add members at any time. Just create your party, set a timeframe for your newsletter, and stay in touch with those closest to you.
